About Ronald

A neighbor first, an advisor second

"I've sat at enough kitchen tables in Tupelo and Amory to know this call is never easy to make. My job is to make everything after it easier."

— Ronald Duke, Founder

Ronald Duke grew up in northeast Mississippi, and he's spent his career here — not in a call center somewhere else, but actually walking the halls of the communities he recommends. He started Assisted Living Finder after watching too many families in Tupelo and Amory struggle through the same thing: a parent's health changes, suddenly there's a decision to make, and nobody in the family has done this before.

He'd seen it happen with his own family, and later with friends and neighbors who called him for advice simply because he'd been through it and they trusted him. Eventually, helping families find the right care stopped being a favor and became the work itself.

What hasn't changed is how he approaches it: no pressure, no pushing a family toward whichever community pays best, and no rushing a decision that deserves time. He listens first, explains plainly, and only recommends a place he'd be comfortable with for his own parents.
