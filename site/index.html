Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes. Assisted Living Finder is funded by the assisted living facilities and retirement villages we refer families to, not by you. There is never a fee, a bill, or a hidden cost to your family.

What's the difference between an assisted living facility and a retirement village?

An assisted living facility provides daily support with meals, medication management, and personal care in a supervised community. A retirement village offers a simpler, maintenance-free lifestyle and community for active seniors who don't need daily care assistance. Ronald can help you figure out which fits your family.

What areas do you serve?

Ronald serves Tupelo, MS, Amory, MS, and 18 surrounding towns and communities across northeast Mississippi, helping families find nearby assisted living facilities and retirement villages.

How fast can we get help?

Most families reach Ronald the same day they call. From that first conversation, tours can often be arranged within days rather than weeks.

Do you get paid by the facilities you refer us to?

Yes, and we're upfront about it. Assisted Living Finder is compensated by the assisted living facilities and retirement villages we refer families to, which is exactly why our service is free to you. Ronald's recommendations are based on fit for your family, not which community pays the most.
